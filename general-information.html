Typical neural network lifecycle is the following:
  1. Adjust neural-classifier:*learn-rate*, neural-classifier:*decay-rate* and neural-classifier:*minibatch-size* parameters if you want.
  2. Create a neural network with neural-classifier:make-neural-network.
  3. Train neural network for N epochs with calling neural-classifier:train-epochN times. You can use neural-classifier:rate function to control the accuracy of your network, so you know where to stop training.
  4. After the net is trained, call neural-classifier:calculate to pass your data through the net.
